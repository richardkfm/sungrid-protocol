Sungrid Protocol — Grid Infrastructure Dossier

Phase 5 · Pixel Mockups

Status: faux pixel art — not a real sprite sheet

Same 7 buildings, redrawn as blocky, hard-edged, limited-palette sprites — the pixel-art feel the schematic dossier didn't have. Each is hand-placed on a 26×18 cell grid, one flat color per cell, no anti-aliasing, in the spirit of a small RTS building cameo.

Still not shippable. These are vector rectangles standing in for pixels, not real indexed-palette .shp sprites in OpenRA's actual format, and they carry no animation frames (idle/damaged/make/dead). Producing those needs either a human pixel artist or an image-generation tool feeding OpenRA's sprite pipeline — neither of which is available in this session. Treat this as "how it could read at a glance," not final art.
