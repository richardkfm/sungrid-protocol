The Phase 5 Seven

SGCRY – SGSNS
SGCRY

Cryptominer

Economy
Cost 1,800
Power −120
Queue Building

Legacy proof-of-work compute, run hot off cheap solar. Not clean the way Solar Array is — and it looks it: rust, salvage, one thin gold cable feeding a rack that was never meant to be green.

Income tiers off GrantConditionOnPowerState — throttles under grid strain, never a flat rate.

SGDAI

Datacenter for AI

Economy / Intel
Cost 1,600
Power −60
Queue Building

Racks serving grid logistics and battlefield analytics — the brains behind smart-grid coordination. Cleaner lines than Cryptominer's, a dome instead of a stack, gold trim instead of rust.

CashTrickler + RevealsShroud + DetectCloaked — economy and intel from one hull, no new trait.

SGDRN

Drone Bay

Logistics
Cost 900
Power −20
Queue Building

A landing shelf bolted onto whatever structure was standing — improvised, not architecture-magazine. The drones matter more than the pad; nothing here reads as permanent.

ProximityExternalCondition grants nearby vehicles a SpeedMultiplier — logistics as an aura.

SGTUR

Grid Defense Turret

Defense
Cost 900
Power −40
Queue Defense

A capacitor coil runs visibly up the mount — charged gold when the grid is healthy, dim when it isn't. It should always read as a weapon first, a power gauge second.

FirepowerMultiplier scales with grid state — weaker under strain, deliberately never offline.

SGREL

Smart Grid Relay

Energy
Cost 600
Power +60
Queue Building

The "balances distant clusters" fantasy is bigger than the building — OpenRA pools power globally already. Drawn honestly small: a secondary feed, not a network.

Flat Power source only; true range-limited pooling stays deferred (see BUILDINGS.md).

SGSHL

Resilience Shelter

Defense
Cost 1,200
Power −50
Queue Building · Limit 1

Hardened concrete, sandbagged at the base, no visible weapon — this building never fights, it just keeps everything around it standing a little longer.

DamageMultiplier aura on nearby structures via ProximityExternalCondition — a comeback building.

SGSNS

Sensor Array

Intelligence
Cost 800
Power −30
Queue Building

A thin mast, three pods at three angles, one blinking status light. Small footprint, wide reach — the building is a symbol for what it does, not a monument.

RevealsShroud + DetectCloaked + RenderDetectionCircle — pure awareness, no combat role.
